Welcome to CS Research Symposium!

SACM and WACM are hosting a virtual research symposium to facilitate connections and conversations between members of the UW-Madison CS community. Graduate and undergraduate students involved in research are invited to present their work through presentations, lightning talks and/or poster sessions. There will also be keynote talks by the faculty members. Presenters will have the chance to win exciting prizes!
