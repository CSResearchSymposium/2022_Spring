Why

Computer Sciences Research Symposium is to facilitate connections and conversations between members of the UW-Madison CS community.
Graduate and undergraduate students involved in research are invited to present their work through presentations, lightning talks and/or poster sessions.

It is a great opportunity to find out what your peers are working on and present your own work, which is one of the key parts of research.
